# Product Overview

CORELINE Smart Spine Corset is a wearable spinal support system engineered to assist the human body rather than restrict it.

Unlike traditional medical braces, CORELINE is designed to:
- work with natural movement
- redistribute mechanical load
- encourage healthier posture habits over time

---

## Target users

CORELINE is designed for a wide range of users, including:

- Office workers and remote professionals
- People with mild to moderate back discomfort
- Athletes and physically active individuals
- Individuals recovering from posture-related strain
- Anyone seeking preventative spinal support

---

## Use cases

- Daily office work and long sitting sessions
- Light to moderate physical activity
- Training and recovery support
- Preventative posture correction
- Lifestyle use under regular clothing

---

## Design philosophy

CORELINE is not a rigid medical brace.

It is a **biomechanically informed support system** that:
- adapts to movement
- maintains comfort over long wear
- avoids excessive compression
- minimizes muscle dependency

---

## Smart future (optional modules)

While the core product functions without electronics, the system is designed for future expansion:

- posture monitoring sensors
- load distribution analytics
- mobile app integration
- personalized recommendations

All smart features will remain optional and modular.
