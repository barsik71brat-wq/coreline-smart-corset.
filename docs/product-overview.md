# Product Overview

The CORELINE Smart Corset is designed to provide passive and adaptive support to the human spine.
It combines an ergonomic external shell with internal layers of aerogel-like smart foam and carbon-fiber reinforcement.
The intended benefits:
- Reduce spinal load during lifting and prolonged sitting
- Improve posture alignment across the corset height
- Provide comfortable everyday wear (breathable, low-profile)
- Enable optional sensor module for future posture analytics

Target users: athletes, office workers, people with chronic low-back pain, rehabilitation patients under guidance.

Editions:
- Early Prototype Edition (physical support, feedback group)
- Standard Edition (production model)
- Sensor Edition (integrated sensors + mobile app)
